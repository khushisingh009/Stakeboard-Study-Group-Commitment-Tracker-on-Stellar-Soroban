# Demo video script (1–2 minutes)

Use two browser profiles (or two devices) to represent two study group members.

1. **(0:00–0:15) Hook** — "This is a study group with actual skin in the
   game. Everyone stakes the same amount, and whoever finishes the syllabus
   splits what the dropouts left behind."

2. **(0:15–0:35) Create & join** — Create a cohort with 4 sessions, join as
   member A with a wallet, show the stake transaction confirm. Switch to
   member B, join too. Show both stakes reflected on the syllabus board.

3. **(0:35–0:55) Check in & close a session** — As member A, check in for
   session 1 via the Check-in view. As the organizer, close session 1 —
   show the live activity feed pick up `MilestoneClosed`, and show member
   B's miss count tick up on the board since they didn't check in.

4. **(0:55–1:15) Drop & finalize** — Close the remaining sessions (member B
   keeps missing), show member B flip to "Dropped" once they exceed
   max_misses. Finalize the cohort and show member A's balance jump by the
   full pool.

5. **(1:15–1:30) Wrap-up** — Resize to phone width to show mobile
   responsiveness, show `cargo test --workspace` passing, and show the
   GitHub Actions CI run green.

Keep narration plain and specific — name what's on screen, not what it's
"powered by."
